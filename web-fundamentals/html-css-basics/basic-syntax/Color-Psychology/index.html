Color Psychology

Red

Constantly Associated with:

  • Love
  • Emotion
  • Energy
  • Anger
  • Danger

When to Use:

  • Food
  • Fashion
  • Entertainment
  • Emergency Services
  • Health

Avoid in:

  • Luxury
  • Environment
  • General Services

Yellow

Constantly Associated with:

  • Happiness
  • Joy
  • Optimism
  • Cowardice

When to use:

  • Illumination
  • Clarity
  • Happiness
  • Attention

Avoid:

  • May be associate to spam or cheap.

Orange

Constantly Associated with:

  • Ambition
  • Heat
  • Caution
  • Fun

When to use:

  • E-commerce
  • Entertainment
  • Call-to-Action

Avoid:

  • Can be exhausting if used too much.
